\section{1. Methodology and Models Used}
We applied time series forecasting to 11 NSE stocks, allocated Rs.10,00,000 in a virtual portfolio on StockGro, and compared predictions against actual market outcomes. Our models include ARIMA, Prophet, LSTM, Exponential Smoothing, and GARCH.
\begin{itemize}[nosep]
    \item \textbf{Dataset:} Yahoo Finance (yfinance), Jan 2021 - Dec 2025, daily.
    \item \textbf{Split:} Training ($<$ Jul 2025) | Testing (Jul-Dec 2025, 125 observations).
\end{itemize}

\section{3. Data Preprocessing Steps (Task 2)}
\begin{itemize}[nosep]
    \item \textbf{Missing values:} Forward-fill for market holidays and trading halts.
    \item \textbf{Stationarity:} Augmented Dickey-Fuller (ADF) test applied; 10 out of 11 stocks required first-order differencing (d=1).
    \item \textbf{Scaling:} Min-Max normalization for LSTM inputs (60-day lookback).
    \item \textbf{STL Decomposition:} Period=63 for trend/seasonal/residual separation.
\end{itemize}

\section{10. Reflections \& Analysis}
Analysis of Errors: Stocks like HDFCBANK, ITC, INFY, TCS show 30-45\% errors due to likely corporate actions (stock splits/bonus issues) between Jan-May 2026. Stocks near Dec 2025 levels (SBIN: 1.23\%, DRREDDY: 0.64\%) had excellent predictions.

\subsection{What Worked}
\begin{itemize}[nosep]
\item ARIMA delivered excellent backtest accuracy (0.77\% avg MAPE)
\item Data-driven stock selection using quantitative screening of 25 candidates
\item Multi-strategy portfolio blending provided diversified allocation
\item GARCH correctly identified high vs low-risk stocks
\end{itemize}

\subsection{What Didn't Work}
\begin{itemize}[nosep]
\item \textbf{Backtest vs live gap:} 0.77\% backtest MAPE vs 20\% live MAPE due to the 5-month extrapolation gap.
\item \textbf{Corporate actions} in HDFCBANK, ITC, INFY changed price levels drastically.
\item \textbf{All-long strategy:} broad market downturn dragged all stocks down.
\end{itemize}

\subsection{What I Would Improve}
\begin{enumerate}[nosep]
\item Retrain models daily on latest data before live predictions
\item Include exogenous variables (Nifty 50, FII/DII flows, USD/INR)
\item Add regime detection for bull/bear market switching
\item Ensemble models weighted by recent per-stock accuracy
\item Stop-loss mechanisms based on GARCH volatility forecasts
\end{enumerate}
